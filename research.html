Our Research 我们的研究

Pioneering research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and synthetic biology, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in biological engineering and computational biology.

Research Overview

Our laboratory is focused on developing innovative approaches that combine artificial intelligence and synthetic biology to solve complex biological problems 我们的实验室专注于开发结合人工智能和合成生物学的创新方法，以解决复杂的生物学问题

The Laboratory for Intelligent Synthetic Biology is at the forefront of research that combines computational methods with wet-lab techniques to design and engineer biological systems with unprecedented precision and functionality.

Our interdisciplinary team brings together expertise in molecular biology, computer science, machine learning, and bioinformatics to address fundamental challenges in synthetic biology and develop new applications in medicine, biotechnology, and environmental sustainability.

We leverage the power of artificial intelligence to predict biological behaviors, optimize designs, and accelerate the engineering cycle, enabling us to create novel biological systems that would be difficult or impossible to develop using traditional approaches alone.

Research Areas

We focus on several key research areas that combine artificial intelligence and synthetic biology 我们专注于几个结合人工智能和合成生物学的关键研究领域

AI for Protein Design

蛋白质设计的人工智能方法

Our team is developing advanced machine learning algorithms to predict and design protein structures with specific functions. We use deep learning approaches to model protein folding dynamics and generate novel protein sequences that fold into desired conformations.

Key applications include the design of enzymes with enhanced catalytic properties, binding proteins for therapeutic and diagnostic purposes, and self-assembling protein structures for drug delivery and nanotechnology applications.

Deep Learning Models

Neural networks for protein structure prediction and sequence generation

Structure-Function Relationships

Understanding how protein structure determines biological function

Learn more about our protein design research

Synthetic Gene Circuits

合成基因电路

We design and engineer complex genetic circuits that can sense, process information, and respond to environmental stimuli in living cells. Our approach combines theoretical modeling with experimental validation to create reliable biological systems with predictable behaviors.

Applications include biosensors for environmental monitoring and medical diagnostics, genetic switches for controlling gene expression, and biological computing systems that can perform logic operations and store information.

Computational Modeling

Mathematical models to predict genetic circuit behavior

Circuit Optimization

AI-based methods to optimize circuit performance and stability

Learn more about our gene circuit research

Microbiome Engineering

微生物组工程

Our research focuses on understanding and engineering microbial communities for applications in medicine, agriculture, and environmental sustainability. We develop computational methods to analyze microbiome data and design interventions to manipulate microbial community composition and function.

Current projects include the design of synthetic consortia for enhanced bioremediation, probiotic formulations for human health, and microbial communities that can improve crop productivity and resilience.

Community Analysis

Multi-omics approaches to study microbial communities

Synthetic Consortia

Designing stable and functional microbial communities

Learn more about our microbiome research

Genome Editing Technologies

基因组编辑技术

We are developing advanced genome editing technologies with improved precision, efficiency, and safety. Our research includes the engineering of CRISPR-Cas systems with enhanced specificity, new base editing approaches, and novel delivery methods for genome editing tools.

We also develop machine learning algorithms to predict off-target effects and optimize guide RNA design, enabling more precise and reliable genome modifications for research and therapeutic applications.

CRISPR Optimization

Engineering CRISPR systems with improved specificity

Off-Target Prediction

AI methods to predict and minimize off-target effects

Learn more about our genome editing research
