Our Vision & Mission

The Laboratory for Intelligent Synthetic Biology is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of synthetic biology through artificial intelligence and computational approaches. Our mission is to develop novel technologies that can address some of the most pressing global challenges in health, energy, and environmental sustainability.

We believe that the convergence of synthetic biology, artificial intelligence, and computational science represents one of the most promising frontiers for scientific discovery and technological innovation. Our interdisciplinary team is working at this exciting intersection to develop new tools and approaches that can transform biological systems for beneficial applications.

By joining our laboratory, you will have the opportunity to work on groundbreaking research projects, collaborate with world-class scientists from diverse disciplines, and contribute to solutions that have the potential to make a significant impact on human health and the environment.

Application Process

How to apply for positions in our laboratory 如何申请我们实验室的职位

Application Submission

Submit your application through our online portal, including your CV, cover letter, research statement, and contact information for three references.

For specific position requirements, please refer to the job description for the position you are interested in.

Initial Screening

Our hiring committee will review all applications and select candidates whose qualifications and research interests align with our laboratory's goals and needs.

Selected candidates will be contacted for an initial interview, typically scheduled within 2-4 weeks after the application deadline.

Interview Process

The interview process typically includes a virtual presentation of your research, followed by individual interviews with members of our laboratory and faculty from related departments.

For some positions, we may invite you for an on-campus visit to meet our team in person and see our facilities.

Decision & Offer

After completing the interview process, our committee will make a final decision and extend an offer to the selected candidate.

We typically inform candidates of our decision within 2 weeks of the final interview. The offer will include details about salary, benefits, start date, and other relevant information.

Postdoctoral Researcher - AI Protein Design

Full-time, 2-year position with possibility of extension

Postdoctoral

About the Position

We are seeking highly motivated Postdoctoral Researchers to join our team working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and protein design. The successful candidate will develop and apply advanced machine learning approaches to design novel proteins with specific functions and properties.

This project is part of a larger effort to develop AI-driven platforms for protein engineering with applications in therapeutics, diagnostics, and biotechnology.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement machine learning models for protein structure prediction and design
  • Collaborate with experimental scientists to validate computational predictions
  • Design and execute computational experiments to optimize protein properties
  • Write manuscripts for publication in peer-reviewed journals
  • Present research findings at conferences and lab meetings

Qualifications

  • PhD in Computer Science, Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, or related field
  • Strong background in machine learning, deep learning, and computational methods
  • Experience with protein structure prediction or design algorithms
  • Proficiency in Python and experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Applications accepted until position is filled

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with molecular dynamics simulations
  • Background in biochemistry or structural biology
  • Previous publications in protein engineering or computational biology

Application Materials

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Cover Letter describing your research interests and qualifications
  • Research Statement (2-3 pages)
  • Contact information for 3 references
