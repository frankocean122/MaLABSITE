About Our Laboratory

The Laboratory for Intelligent Synthetic Biology is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of synthetic biology through the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques.

Our multidisciplinary team combines expertise in molecular biology, computer science, and bioinformatics to develop novel approaches for designing and engineering biological systems with unprecedented precision and efficiency.
