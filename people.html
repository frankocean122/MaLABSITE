Our Collaborative Culture

At the Laboratory for Intelligent Synthetic Biology, we foster a collaborative and inclusive environment where diverse perspectives are valued and innovation thrives.

Our team members come from backgrounds including molecular biology, computer science, machine learning, bioinformatics, chemistry, and engineering, creating a rich interdisciplinary atmosphere that enables us to tackle complex challenges from multiple angles.

We believe that the most groundbreaking discoveries happen at the intersection of disciplines, and we actively encourage knowledge sharing, creative thinking, and open collaboration both within our team and with external partners.

Team Diversity & Inclusion

We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive research environment 我们致力于培养多元化和包容性的研究环境

At the Laboratory for Intelligent Synthetic Biology, we believe that diversity drives innovation. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where researchers from all backgrounds can thrive and contribute to our mission.

Our laboratory values diversity in all its forms, including but not limited to race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, age, disability, and socioeconomic background. We actively promote equity and inclusion in our recruitment, mentoring, and research practices.

We recognize that diverse teams produce more creative solutions and better science. By bringing together individuals with different perspectives and experiences, we can tackle complex research challenges more effectively and develop technologies that benefit diverse populations worldwide.

Our Commitments

  • Creating an inclusive environment where all team members feel valued and respected
  • Promoting diversity through recruitment and retention efforts
  • Providing mentorship and support to underrepresented groups in STEM
  • Organizing workshops and events focused on diversity and inclusion
  • Continuously evaluating and improving our diversity and inclusion practices
